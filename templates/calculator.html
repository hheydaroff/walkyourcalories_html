How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter Calories: Input the number of calories you want to burn.
  2. Input Your Weight: Enter your current weight in kilograms for accurate calculations.
  3. Choose Your Pace: Select your walking pace (slow, moderate, or fast) to fine-tune the calculation.
  4. Get Your Results: Click "Calculate Steps" to see how many steps you need to take to burn the specified calories.

Tips for Accurate Results

  • Use your current weight for the most precise calculations.
  • Be honest about your walking pace for realistic step estimates.
  • Remember that these are estimates and individual results may vary.
  • Consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new exercise regimen.
